Symptoms of PCOS

Understanding the signs your body may be giving you

Common Symptoms

PCOS symptoms vary from person to person. Some may experience a few symptoms while others see multiple. These symptoms usually appear due to hormonal imbalance and irregular ovulation.

  • Irregular or missed periods
  • Acne and oily skin
  • Excess facial/body hair (hirsutism)
  • Sudden weight gain or difficulty losing weight
  • Hair thinning or hair fall
  • Darkened skin patches (neck, armpits)
  • Fertility problems

Why symptoms differ?

PCOS affects each woman differently based on hormone levels, lifestyle and genetics. Early detection and monitoring can help prevent long-term complications.
