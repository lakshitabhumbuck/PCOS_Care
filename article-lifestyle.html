Lifestyle & Self-Care for PCOS

Habits that help maintain hormonal balance

Why lifestyle matters?

PCOS is highly connected to metabolism and hormones. A balanced lifestyle reduces symptoms and improves overall health.

Healthy Lifestyle tips

  • Exercise 30 minutes daily
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in protein & fiber
  • Reduce sugar, fried food & processed carbs
  • Manage stress with meditation or yoga
  • Maintain good sleep habits
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day

Long-term benefits

Following these steps improves skin, weight, mental health, and fertility in the long run.
